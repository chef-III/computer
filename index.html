Welcome Captain!

This project is just for fun. I try to make my own ship computer like the one in the old Star Trek The Next generation series.

The main goals are:

  • controlling lights and other components of my home with voice commands
  • checkout new calls, e-mails, etc with voice command and text to speak
  • get information about e.g. weather, tram or bus
  • have a cool thing to impress friends :D

Later I'm planning to enhance the system with a nice LCARS interface on a big touchscreen...

Feel free to clone or download this and play with it.
